# Pipeline Fundamentals Capstone (Starter)

## Assignment Title
**What Is CI to You?**

## Objective
Write a **minimum 300-word essay** in your own words explaining:

1. What Continuous Integration (CI) means to you.
2. How the CI process works at a practical level.
3. Why CI is important in the Software Development Cycle.

This capstone is designed to confirm your understanding of CI concepts from this module and your ability to communicate technical ideas clearly.

---

## Prompt
In a well-organized essay, respond to the following:

> **“What is CI to you?”**

Your response should describe CI as both a technical workflow and a team practice.

---

## Required Essay Content
Your essay must include all of the following:

- A clear definition of CI in your own words.
- An explanation of the CI flow (for example: commit, push, automated build, tests, feedback).
- Why CI helps teams reduce bugs and integration problems.
- How CI supports collaboration, code quality, and release confidence.
- At least one real or realistic example scenario of CI in action.

---

## Format Requirements
- **Minimum length:** 300 words
- **Maximum length:** 500 words (recommended)
- **File name:** `ci-essay.md`
- **File type:** Markdown (`.md`)
- Use complete sentences and paragraph structure.
- Write in professional, original language.

---

## Submission Instructions
1. In this `Starter` folder, create a new file named `ci-essay.md`.
2. Write your essay directly in that file.
3. Proofread for spelling, grammar, and clarity.
4. Confirm your essay is at least 300 words.
5. Submit your repository/assignment according to your class submission process.

---

## Suggested Essay Structure (Optional)
You may follow this structure if helpful:

1. **Introduction (1 paragraph):** What CI means to you.
2. **Body Paragraph 1:** How CI works in a pipeline.
3. **Body Paragraph 2:** Why CI matters in the software development cycle.
4. **Body Paragraph 3:** Example of how CI improves a project/team outcome.
5. **Conclusion:** Final reflection on CI value.

---

## Evaluation Rubric (100 points)
- **CI Understanding (30 pts):** Accurate explanation of CI concepts.
- **Process Explanation (20 pts):** Clear description of CI workflow steps.

- **Clarity and Organization (15 pts):** Logical structure, readability, and flow.
- **Mechanics and Format (10 pts):** Grammar, spelling, word count, and required filename.
